Forex Basics

Understanding the fundamentals of Forex trading.

Key Points

  • Forex, or foreign exchange, is the global marketplace for currency trading where currencies are exchanged.
  • Trading pairs like EUR/USD represent the relative value of one currency compared to another, forming the basis for forex trades.
  • Forex operates 24 hours a day, 5 days a week, offering flexibility for traders in different time zones.
  • Major participants include central banks, commercial banks, institutional investors, corporations, and retail traders.
  • Forex trading is highly liquid, meaning large volumes of currency can be traded with minimal impact on price.
  • Popular forex strategies include scalping, day trading, swing trading, and position trading, each suited to different trader profiles.
  • The forex market is influenced by geopolitical events, economic data releases, and changes in interest rates.
